# ACCOUNT SETUP COMPLETE GUIDE

This guide provides detailed step-by-step instructions for setting up fresh accounts on Apple, Google, GitHub, and Notion, including 2FA setup, recovery codes, and security best practices.

## Apple Account Setup

### Step 1: Creating an Apple ID
1. Go to the [Apple ID website](https://appleid.apple.com/).
2. Click on "Create Your Apple ID."  
3. Fill in your details, including your name, email, and password.
4. Select your security questions and answers.
5. Click on "Continue" to complete account creation.

### Step 2: Setting Up 2FA
1. Sign in to your Apple ID account page.
2. In the Security section, click "Edit."
3. Turn on Two-Factor Authentication.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to verify your number and complete setup.

### Step 3: Saving Recovery Codes
- Write down your recovery codes and keep them in a safe place.
- **Checklist:** 
  - [ ] Apple ID created
  - [ ] 2FA enabled
  - [ ] Recovery codes saved

## Google Account Setup

### Step 1: Creating a Google Account
1. Go to the [Google Account creation page](https://accounts.google.com/signup).
2. Enter your information: name, email address, and password.
3. Click on "Next" and follow the prompts to complete account setup.

### Step 2: Setting Up 2FA
1. Go to your Google Account Security settings.
2. Under "Signing in to Google," select 2-Step Verification.
3. Click on "Get Started" and follow the prompts.
4. Verify your phone number, then choose your verification method (text or app).

### Step 3: Saving Recovery Codes
- After setting up 2FA, Google will offer backup codes. Save these codes.
- **Checklist:** 
  - [ ] Google account created
  - [ ] 2FA enabled
  - [ ] Recovery codes saved

## GitHub Account Setup

### Step 1: Creating a GitHub Account
1. Go to the [GitHub website](https://github.com/join).
2. Fill in your username, email, and password.
3. Verify your email to activate your account.

### Step 2: Setting Up 2FA
1. Click on your profile picture and go to Settings.
2. In the Security section, select "Two-factor authentication."
3. Choose your authentication method (SMS or authenticator app).
4. Follow the prompts to complete setup.

### Step 3: Saving Recovery Codes
- GitHub will provide recovery codes. Keep them in a secure location.
- **Checklist:** 
  - [ ] GitHub account created
  - [ ] 2FA enabled
  - [ ] Recovery codes saved

## Notion Account Setup

### Step 1: Creating a Notion Account
1. Visit the [Notion website](https://www.notion.so/signup).
2. Enter your email address and follow the confirmation link.
3. Choose a password and set up your workspace.

### Step 2: Setting Up 2FA
1. In Notion, go to Settings & Members.
2. Scroll to the Security section and enable Two-Factor Authentication.
3. Link your authenticator app or phone number.

### Step 3: Saving Recovery Codes
- Notion will send you recovery codes upon enabling 2FA. Store them safely.
- **Checklist:** 
  - [ ] Notion account created
  - [ ] 2FA enabled
  - [ ] Recovery codes saved

## Security Best Practices
- Always use a strong, unique password for each account.
- Do not share your passwords or recovery codes with anyone.
- Regularly review your account security settings.
